About The College

EST. 1975

Parvathaneni Brahmayya Siddhartha College of Arts & Science, established in 1975 with 200 students and 15 faculty members, was the first institution started by the Academy. It gained autonomy for undergraduate courses in 1988 under UGC and Acharya Nagarjuna University.

Today, the college has grown into a leading institution with 2,798 students and 114 faculty members. Recognized by the UGC for excellence in teaching, research, and extension activities, it offers diverse academic programs that foster both knowledge and character. The college emphasizes holistic education, preparing students to excel in academics, extracurricular activities, and ethical values.

Over its 40+ years, it has introduced various restructured, vocational, and postgraduate courses, producing thousands of graduates in Arts, Sciences, Commerce, and Management. Committed to continuous improvement, the college pursues NAAC re-accreditation to maintain high standards.

Enter to learn, leave to serve, and live as a Siddharthian

The college is a hub for skill development, career guidance, and personal growth.

Our department is committed to nurturing future data scientists and AI specialists equipped with cutting-edge skills and ethical understanding to solve real-world challenges.

The Department of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at PB Siddhartha College of Arts & Science offers three specialized programs—Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Analytics, each with an intake of 50 students.

Our programs provide a strong foundation in machine learning, deep learning, data analytics, and AI-driven problem-solving, integrating mathematics, statistics, and computer science. We focus on hands-on learning, industry collaboration, and research, preparing students to tackle real-world challenges in AI and data-driven decision-making.

Through partnerships with industry, government, and research organizations, we offer internships, collaborative projects, and practical experience, bridging the gap between theory and application. Our department is committed to innovation and excellence, aspiring to be a leader in AI and data science education in the region.
